Ameens Travels

Hey there ! my name is Ameen Jackson and I have an affinity for traveling. So , I compiled all the places that I’ve visited and I would like to share them with you .

The places that I have traveled to are :

  • Memphis, TN
  • Kansas city, MO
  • Jacksonville, FL

St.louis, MO

I was born and raised in St.louis , MO and have many fond memories of my upbringing in the gateway to the west . Some of those memories include ; visiting the arch , playing my heart out at the city museum, and spending time at the nationally recognized St.louis Zoo . These memories sparked my interest in visiting other places and seeing what they have to offer .

Memphis, TN

Memphis , TN is an another great city that I’ve visited a ton of times and lived for a short period of time . I have lots of family in Memphis and hold many unforgettable memories . Some of those memorable moments are : attending Memphis in may festivities , walking down wonderful Beale street , and graduating middle school in Memphis. This a one of a kind city that I’ll continue to visit over time .

Kansas City, MO

Kansas City , is a one of a kind city , located in Missouri that Has a ton to offer . My travels to Kansas City consisted of visiting “worlds of fun “ amusement park , “oceans of fun” water park , and the beautiful down town . Kansas City also has a delicious food scene that will keep me visiting for years to come

Jacksonville, FL

Jacksonville , FL is the most recent city I visited located in north eastern Florida . This city sit along the Atlantic coast and has a ton of beaches and sea life adventures. While visiting, I had an awesome time on the beach , attending festivals, and exploring the wild life in the Jacksonville zoo . The weather , great food , and warm hospitality made me a big fan of the city .
